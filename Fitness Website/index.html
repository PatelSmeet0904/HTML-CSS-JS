Features

Certified Trainers

We have a team of best certified trainers. Our personal trainers are here to help all members seeking to make the most out of their workouts. A personal trainer can help you establish a timeline for realistic goals and break them down into smaller components. They are very friendly, knowledgeable and highly motivated in helping you achieve your fitness goals. On your first session, you can expect to share a little bit about yourself, your medical history and what your lifestyle consists of.

Wide Space

Our 22,000 square foot space allows us to carry everything you would look for in a gym! We have filled the gym with top of the line machines, weights and workout accessories to help all of our members reach their personal goals. We have a large class room that holds 80+ classes a week featuring zumba, yoga, pilates, muscle conditioning, trx and much more. Our class areas are big enough to allow no pre-registration, this makes working out a worry free and easy task.

Flexible Time

Being a 24/7 gym is one of our best amenities because we can offer our members unrestricted freedom of access from sun up to sun down and everywhere in between. You no longer have to worry about cramming in gym time before we close! The flexibility of having access to the gym 24/7 makes it easier for your to plan your schedule and cut down on the excuses of not being able to go to the gym.

Services

Yoga classes

Yoga practice is a great way to strengthen the bond between your mind and your body. A simple yoga practice can increase your levels of concentration, alleviate symptoms of anxiety, help you sleep better at time, increase flexibility.

Zumba classes

Zumba is a dance based workout. If you “don’t like working out” – you obviously have not tried shaking your booty in a zumbaclass! Zumba will change your life one beat at a time.

Aerobic classes

Aerobic classes are meant to make you move, sweat and get a little out of breath. Our certified instructors know how to make you move and know exactly when to start pushing you.

Personal Training

A personal trainer can help you establish a timeline for realistic goals and break them down into smaller components. They will motivate you to keep coming and to keep pushing. They are like your personal cheerleader for an hour!

Steam & Sauna Rooms

We offers a relaxing spa area equipped with steam, sauna, shower and beauty station. The steam room has the ability to help clear skin, improve circulation and open up the sinuses.

Changing room & Lockers

If you are coming at gym from your work or you want to go at work after gym then you can change your cloth & also take a shower in Changing room. By using Lockers you can secure your important things.
